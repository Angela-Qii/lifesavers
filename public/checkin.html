Section 1 - Filling PASI Score

The Psoriasis Area and Severity Index (PASI) score is a tool dermatologists use to classify psoriasis and help determine treatment. It helps classify severity of your psoriasis.

In general, a PASI score of 5 to 10 is considered moderate disease, and a score over 10 is considered severe.
